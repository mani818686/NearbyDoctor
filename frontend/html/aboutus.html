-OUR MISSION-

we care for your health

Life is becoming too busy to get medical appointments in person and to maintain a proper health care.
The main idea of this work is to provide ease and comfort to patients while taking appointment from doctors
and it also resolves the problems that the patients has to face while making an appointment. The website application
“NEARBYDOCTOR” acts as a client whereas the database containing the doctor’s details, patient’s details and appointment details is maintained by a website that we designed.
“NEARBYDOCTOR” is a web application which help patients to reach their respective specialized doctors to solve their health complications in an appropriate manner.
Through this application patients can reach out many doctors . In the same way doctors can reach out many patients and can gain their experience.
Through this application patient can search for a doctor around his/her location .
By this way they consult doctor in a simple and easy way.
